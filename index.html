Our Story

My name is Rosinda Pinon, Administrative Support, General Finance Manager, and Co-Founder of American Prime Solutions, a Texas-based, fully bilingual, multi-trade service company built on integrity and powered by American excellence.

We specialize in construction, electrical, HVAC, plumbing, and TEC services, supporting both residential and commercial projects. Our team handles everything from full remodels and renovations to technical installations and urgent repairs, delivering safe, durable, and efficient results with a strong focus on professionalism and transparency.

American Prime Solutions is fully equipped with professional-grade tools and machinery, allowing us to self-perform a wide range of scopes efficiently and safely. We’ve had the privilege of working with commercial, retail, hospitality, and government clients across Texas, including Marble Slab Creamery, Motel 8, Milano Nail Salon & Spa, Harris County, and various commercial plazas and private property owners.

In 2025, our craftsmanship was recognized by HGTV, where we won the Battling of the Bathroom award for a newly built bathroom project—an acknowledgment of our attention to detail, quality, and innovation.

We would welcome the opportunity to become your construction service provider and we are always happy to provide additional information such as insurance, certifications, references, or to discuss how our services could align with your needs.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to the possibility of working together.

Best regards,
— Rosinda Pinon
